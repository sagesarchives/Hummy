# What is Hummy?
Hummy is a digital tool that utilizes vibration haptics to guide users in humming sessions to help them better connect with their bodies.

# Why Hummy?
Many people navigate through life with an unbalanced mind-body connection representing itself in the form of physical issues like chronic headaches, high blood pressure, and muscle tension.

# How does it work?
Users will choose between a one minute, two minute, or three minute session, and hum along to the vibration haptic. Users are instructed to focus their attention on the buzzing vibration they feel from their hums as a form of meditative practice.
